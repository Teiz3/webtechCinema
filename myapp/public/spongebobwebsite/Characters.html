SpongeBob SquarePants

The main protagonist is SpongeBob SquarePants. He is a yellow sponge that works as a chef at the Krusty Krab, Bikini Bottom's fast food restaurant. He lives in a pineapple, and is neighbours with his best friend Patrick and his co-worker Squidward. SpongeBob is very energetic, childlike and pure of heart, which makes him well known throughout the entire town. In order to safe his boss, Mr. Krabs, he is tasked with retrieving King Neptune's crown from across the ocean, with help from Patrick.

Patrick Star

The other character that we spend most of the movie with is Patrick Star. Patrick is a pink seastar who lives under a rock and is jobless. He spends most of his days at home, either watching TV or waiting until SpongeBob comes home so they can go jellyfishing. As best friend to SpongeBob he joins him on his quest to retrieve the stolen crown of King Neptune.

Eugene Krabs

Eugene H. Krabs (Mr. Krabs) is a greedy crab who owns the Krusty Krab fast food restaurant. He is mainly driven by money and would make use of every exploit in order to get as much money as he can. Mr. Krabs has just opened up a 2nd Krusty Krab restaurant right beside the first one. He is framed by his nemesis Plankton for stealing King Neptune's crown, and frozen as an ice cube by King Neptune himself.

Sheldon Plankton

Sheldon Plankton, often called by just his last name, is the main antagonist of the movie. He's the owner of the Chum Bucket, a competitive fast food restaurant directly across the street from the Krusty Krab. Plankton's main goal is to steal the Krabby Patty's secret formula in order to improve the food of his unseccessful restautant. He also has a robotic wife named Karen.

King Neptune

King Neptune is the ruler of the ocean. He shows a lot of tyranny in his rule, smiting mortals who talk back at him. Neptune's crown is very important to him, as it's not only a status symbol, but also helps cover up his balding head. After finding his crown stolen with a note left behind stating that it was Mr. Krabs that stole it, he went to the Krusty Krab in order to fry him. He got talked out of it by SpongeBob and his daughter Mindy, and gave SpongeBob 6 days to find and return his crown.

Princess Mindy

Princess Mindy is the daughter of King Neptune. Unlike her father, Mindy shows a lot more compassion towards the average sealife, even learning everyone's names. Mindy helps out SpongeBob and Patrick throughout their task, such as giving them a bag of air to get back safely.
