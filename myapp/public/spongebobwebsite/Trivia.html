David Hasselhoff

A 14 foot replica of David Hasselhoff was made for the movie, so the cameramen could film without straining Hasselhoff. The replica was later auctionned off in 2021 after being in Hasselhoff's posession for 17 years.

The End

The SpongeBob SquarePants Movie was intended as the end of the show, but after success of the movie the showrunners announced another season. Every depiction after is set before the events of the movie, leaving the movie as the canon ending.

King Neptune

King Neptune was redesigned for the movie was due to a firewall between Paramount and Nickelodeon. This resulted in elements of the movie not being allowed to be used in the main show. After the firewall was lifted, King Neptune's crown made several appearances in the show.

Nemo

The original cover-up plot of the film had SpongeBob going to rescue Patrick from a fisherman in Florida. This was a reference to Finding Nemo (2003) and was later revealed by Tom Kenny to be just a joke plot which he made up to keep the fans busy.

Inflatable

For promotion material of the movie, about 5000 giant inflatable SpongeBobs were placed on top of Burger King restaurants throughout the United States. A number of these inflatables have been reported to be stolen. They also recieved a ransom note stating: "We have SpongeBob. Give us 10 Krabby Patties fries and milkshakes!"
